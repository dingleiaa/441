Who We Are

We have reformed the teaching building

The university teaching building is the main place for students to learn and teachers to teach, and is an important component of the campus. This teaching building has a solemn and grand appearance, adopting a classic architectural style, showcasing a strong academic atmosphere and cultural heritage. The internal facilities of the teaching building are complete and the layout is reasonable. The floor height is moderate, with sufficient lighting, providing a comfortable learning and working environment for students and teachers. Each classroom is equipped with advanced multimedia equipment and intelligent teaching systems, facilitating interactive teaching and autonomous learning for teachers and students. In addition, the teaching building also emphasizes humanized design and services. There are multiple lounges, discussion rooms, and self-study areas in the building for students to freely communicate and learn. At the same time, the management team of the teaching building provides 24-hour services to ensure that the learning and living needs of teachers and students are met. In short, university teaching buildings are important places for students to learn and teachers to teach. They not only provide high-quality teaching resources and learning environments, but also serve as a cradle for cultivating students' comprehensive qualities and innovative abilities.

Our History

  • 1993  -

  • The Australian College of Health Care was founded in 1993 and is an institution that provides high-quality healthcare education. The school is committed to cultivating healthcare professionals with professional knowledge and skills to meet the demands of society for healthcare. Since its establishment, the Australian University of Health and Nursing has been committed to improving the quality of health and nursing education, continuously improving and perfecting educational curricula and teaching methods. The school enjoys a good reputation in Australia and internationally, providing students with an excellent platform for health and nursing education. In the past few decades, the Australian University of Health and Nursing has undergone multiple changes and developments. The school has gradually developed from a small-scale educational institution to a comprehensive health and nursing college with multiple campuses and majors. At the same time, the school actively cooperates and exchanges with international health and nursing education institutions, providing students with a broader academic perspective and international exchange opportunities. In summary, as a long-standing healthcare education institution, the Australian University of Health and Nursing has trained a large number of outstanding healthcare professionals in Australia and internationally. The school will continue to strive to improve the quality of education and contribute to cultivating more healthcare professionals with professional knowledge and skills.
